Hoods are the unrecognized heroes of kitchen appliances. They don't often get much recognition, but a kitchen exhaust hood can make the difference between a smoky, smelly cooking experience, and an enjoyable one. The primary purpose of an oven hood is to vent or filter the air above your cooking area, but they also help protect the underside of your wood cabinets from harmful steam and the buildup of grease. A chimney hood is a great choice for those without cabinets above their stovetop, while an island hood provides ventilation for cooking areas that are not against a wall. Finally, for those that don't want a hood at all, a downdraft offers similar functionality in low-profile design.

Abt has a large selection of wall hoods to fit a variety of cabinet arrangements, from top brands like GE, Jenn-Air and Kitchenaid.
